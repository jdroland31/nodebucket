Nodebucket is an organization tool for all kinds of people: busy moms, teachers, students, professionals and more. You can create and edit tasks to keep track of your important real-world responsibilities, then move finished tasks into the ‘Done’ column through a simple drag-and-drop interface. When you no longer need to keep track of your finished tasks you can either delete them individually or clear the ‘Done’ column using the trash can icon. When you are finished using the application you can simply sign out with the Log Out button in the upper right corner. To resume, you can log in with your user ID in the Log In screen.
